But it gets kind of messy to eat a sandwich without any bread. Your readers do not know what to do with all the evidence evidence evidence you have given them. So, the top slice of bread (the first sentence of the paragraph) explains the topic (or controlling idea) of the paragraph. And, the bottom slice (the last sentence of the paragraph) tells the reader how the paragraph relates to the broader argument.
